1Introduction & Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how CLNIQ ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects digital personal data through our web-based Patient Management System (PMS).

  • Applicability
    This policy applies to all digital personal data collected online or digitized offline via the CLNIQ platform.
  • Hosted Environment
    CLNIQ is hosted on Microsoft Azure India regions (Central, South, and West), ensuring 100% data residency within India.

2Categories of Data Collected

We collect only the information necessary to provide clinical and administrative services.

  • Patient Data (Data Principal)
    Full name, age, contact details, medical history, prescriptions, diagnostic reports, and ABHA ID.
  • Physician Data (Data Fiduciary)
    Full name, medical registration numbers (NMC/State Council), clinic address, and professional qualifications.
  • Technical Data
    IP address, browser type, and access logs for security and auditing purposes.

3Lawful Basis & Purpose of Processing

Data is processed solely for the following specified purposes:

  • Medical Treatment
    Enabling physicians to manage patient records, appointments, and prescriptions.
  • Administrative Services
    Billing, appointment reminders via SMS/Email, and patient portal access.
  • Legal Compliance
    Fulfilling statutory requirements under the National Medical Commission (NMC) and the DPDP Act 2023.
  • Emergency Use
    Processing data without prior consent during life-threatening medical emergencies, as permitted under Section 7 of the DPDP Act.

5Data Security & Safeguards

As your Data Processor, CLNIQ implements "Reasonable Security Safeguards" to prevent unauthorized access or breaches:

Encryption

AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit for all clinical and administrative data.

Access Control

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) and Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) across all user roles.

Audit Logs

Immutable logs of all data access activities, maintained for a minimum of one year.

Cloud Security

Azure-managed firewalls, DDoS protection, and continuous vulnerability assessments.

6Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • No Commercial Sale
    CLNIQ does not sell, rent, or trade patient data to third parties for marketing or advertising ‚Äî ever.
  • Authorized Sub-processors
    We share data with Microsoft Azure (Infrastructure) and authorized SMS/Email gateways solely for service delivery.
  • Legal Disclosure
    We may disclose data only if required by a valid court order or government authority under Indian law.

7Data Retention & Deletion

  • Retention
    Medical records are retained for 3 years as per NMC guidelines, unless a different period is mandated by law.
  • Deletion
    Upon withdrawal of consent or fulfilment of purpose, data is securely erased or anonymized unless legal obligations require retention.
